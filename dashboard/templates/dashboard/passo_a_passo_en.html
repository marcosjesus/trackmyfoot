Athlete Guide - Track My Foot

Learn how to use the platform to upload and view your performance

1. Log in to your account

  • Go to: www.trackmyfoot.com
  • Click on Login
  • Enter your username and password

2. Upload your PDF files

  • In the top menu, click My Performance
  • Then click on Upload Files
  • Select one or more PDF files generated by your GPS (e.g., PlayerMaker)
  • Click on Process Files

3. View your performance charts

  • After processing, go to My Performance again
  • See your charts: Heat Map, Performance Radar, Before and After, and more

4. Receive feedback from coaches

  • Coaches can see your data and send direct messages
  • Messages appear in your Performance section

5. Share your performance

  • Click Generate Shareable Link
  • Copy and share the link with friends, coaches, or on social media
